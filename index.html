Abank Village Guest House is a welcoming retreat with 50 comfortable rooms spread across multiple blocks, each thoughtfully designed to suit every kind of traveller — whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or an extended stay in Kumasi.

From our cozy standard rooms to spacious family suites with private kitchens, every guest enjoys warm, attentive service alongside modern comforts. We're proud to offer a full restaurant, professional conference facility, and a calm, secure environment — all under one roof in the heart of Emena.
